Why geothermal

The ground is the
furnace. We just
borrow the heat.

A geothermal system moves heat instead of making it. In winter, it pulls warmth from the ground into your home. In summer, it puts it back. The earth does most of the work — we just engineer the loop that taps into it.

How we work

Four steps. About six weeks
from first call to first kWh.

  1. 01 · STEP

    Site visit

    A free 60-minute walk-through. We look at your mechanical room, lot, and electrical service. No pressure, no quote on the spot.

  2. 02 · STEP

    Design & rebates

    We size the loop and equipment, file your CleanBC and federal Greener Homes paperwork, and hand you a fixed price.

  3. 03 · STEP

    Drill & install

    Boreholes in 2–3 days. Indoor equipment in 2–4 more. We patch landscaping and clean up like we were never there.

  4. 04 · STEP

    Live with it

    Quiet operation, lower bills, 10-year parts and labour, and remote monitoring on every system we install.
